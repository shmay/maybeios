XMaybe

Maybe allows you to see who of your friends & family are currently at your favorite spots.

Are they there? Yes / No / Maybe

Pro Tip: Turn Wi-Fi ON in your iOS settings for good location tracking.

FAQ

Who can invite people to spots?
Only the creator of the spot.
How does Maybe work?
When you join or create a spot, Maybe creates a Geo-fence on your phone at that spot. When you enter or leave that Geo-fence, Maybe transmits the YES/NO binary data to our server.
Is Maybe secure?
Yes. All data is sent through SSL, and we've gone to extra measures to keep your location status safe from any would-be hackers.
Does Maybe track my location?
Sort of. It only knows if you've entered or left a Geo-fence. You can disable/enable these Geo-fences at anytime.
Does Maybe keep a history of my location?
No. We only store the absolutely necessary information - whether you are currently within a Geofence or not (if we even know that)
